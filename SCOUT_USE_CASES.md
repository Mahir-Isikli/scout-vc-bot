# Scout — VC Deal Flow Assistant

Scout is an AI-powered Slack bot that lives inside your team's existing Slack workspace and acts as a shared deal flow brain. No new tools to learn. No context switching.

---

## Use Cases

### 1. Capture Deals Instantly, From Anywhere
Drop a quick message like _"Just met with Acme AI — Series A, $10M raise, founder is Sarah Chen, B2B SaaS in logistics"_ and Scout parses it into a structured deal record automatically. No forms, no CRM data entry.

### 2. Search Your Pipeline in Plain English
Ask _"What fintech deals are we tracking at Seed stage?"_ or _"Show me everything we sourced this month"_ and get a clean, formatted list back — without opening a spreadsheet or CRM.

### 3. Deep Enrichment on Any Company
Ask Scout to enrich a deal and it will go research the company on the web — founding team background, recent news, funding history, competitors — and attach it directly to the deal record.

### 4. Shared Team Intelligence
Every deal your team adds is visible to everyone. No more deals living in one partner's inbox or notes app. When someone asks _"Have we seen anything in climate tech?"_, Scout knows.

### 5. Pipeline Stats on Demand
Ask _"What does our pipeline look like?"_ and get a live breakdown — deals by stage, sector, source, and recency — without waiting for an analyst to pull a report.

### 6. Meeting & Context Lookup
Query past interactions: _"What do we know about the founder at NovaTech?"_ or _"When did we last touch base with this company?"_ Scout surfaces the context so you walk into every meeting prepared.
